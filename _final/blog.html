Mitch

  • Terrier
  • Male
  • 1 y/o
  • 20 lbs

Mitch was originally adopted from Rover rescue in the late summer of 2018. We kept in touch with his owner, thinking all was good with Mitch. In March of 2019 we received a call that broke our heart. Mitch was in a shelter in DENVER COLORADO! So what do we do, we fly out to Denver to bail him and drive him back home to Los Angeles. Mitch is such a love, a bit shy, super loyal, and a bit oversized lap dog.
